FOODIES

PIZZA

Pizza is a dish of Italian origin consisting of a usually round, flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients. which is then baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven.

BIRIYANI

Chicken Biryani is a delicious savory rice dish that is loaded with spicy marinated chicken, caramelized onions, and flavorful saffron rice.

BURGER

A burger is a patty of ground beef grilled and placed between two halves of a bun. Slices of raw onion, lettuce, bacon, mayonnaise, and other ingredients add flavor. Burgers are considered an American food but are popular around the world.

GRILLED CHICKEN

Grilled Chicken is a very popular recipe made with chicken, hung curd and spices like cumin powder and black pepper. It is a perfect chicken recipe for a quick meal and will be loved by everyone for its amazing flavours.
